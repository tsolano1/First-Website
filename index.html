Regida Short Bio!-Fuerza Regida is an American regional Mexican band from San Bernardino, California. The band consists of lead vocalist Jesús Ortíz Paz, backing vocalist and twelve-string guitarist Samuel Jáimez, rhythm guitarist Khrystian Ramos, sousaphone player José "Pelón" García and tololoche player Moisés López. Formed in 2015 as a cover band, they later released a song titled "Radicamos en South Central", which would go viral in 2018 and would lead to a signing of a contract with Lumbre Music, where they later released their first live album En Vivo Puros Corridos (2018).
